What We're Building

Facility Plans
  • Permanent chapter facility at the University of Kentucky
  • Housing capacity for 30–50 brothers
  • Dedicated study rooms and common areas
  • Alumni gathering and event space
  • Operated through 501(c)(3) investment vehicle
  • Long-term asset for the Omega Chapter Foundation

Chris von Allmen and Travis Petty are leading the Housing Corp effort. Darin Holland coordinates alumni input, and Jeff Ashley of Pike National advises on the fundraising structure. The full investment model deck is available upon request.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Who manages the housing campaign?

The Housing Corp and Alumni Association oversee all campaign activity. Chris von Allmen and Travis Petty are the primary leads.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes — gifts are made through the Omega Chapter 501(c)(3) foundation and are fully tax-deductible.

When will construction begin?

Construction timing is TBD and will depend on campaign milestones, site selection, and Housing Corp approvals.

Can I see the investment model?

Yes — email Chris von Allmen at cvonallmen@gmail.com to request the full Housing Corp investment deck.

How can alumni get involved beyond donating?

Join a Housing Corp or Alumni Association meeting to contribute ideas and direction. Contact us to get on the agenda.
