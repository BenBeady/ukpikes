Omega Chapter

A Legacy Built at UK

Pi Kappa Alpha's Omega Chapter has been a cornerstone of Greek life at the University of Kentucky for generations. Our brothers have gone on to lead in business, public service, athletics, and their communities — carrying the values of Pike with them.

We are actively collecting photos, stories, and memorabilia to build a comprehensive digital archive. If you have something to contribute, we want to hear from you.

Did You Know?
  • ✦ Pi Kappa Alpha was founded on March 1, 1868 at the University of Virginia.
  • ✦ The Omega Chapter is among the organization's historic chapters, with deep roots at UK.
  • ✦ Pike Nationals has digitized chapter records searchable at the national archive — see what's already available for Omega.

Help Build the Record

Share Your Memories

We're collecting old photos, composite photos, pledge class rosters, event programs, newspaper clippings, and any other Pike memorabilia you might have tucked away. Every contribution helps tell the full story of Omega Chapter.

Jeff Heimer has graciously volunteered to help gather historical information. If you'd like to connect or have materials to share, reach out to the alumni office.
