Mentor / Mentee Program

The Omega Chapter connects alumni professionals with current brothers looking for career guidance. Whether it's resume review, interview prep, or networking introductions, our brothers are here for each other.

The program is managed through our LinkedIn page — follow us and reach out to get matched with an active brother.

Mentorship Areas
  • Career development & resume review
  • Interview prep & mock interviews
  • Job placement & networking
  • Graduate school guidance
  • Entrepreneurship & startups
