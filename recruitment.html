Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need connections to attend rush events?

No. All rush events are open to any University of Kentucky student. Just show up.

Is there a GPA requirement?

Yes — a minimum 2.5 GPA is required to receive a bid. We take scholarship seriously.

What is the weekly time commitment?

Chapter meets once per week. Additional events and programs are available but largely optional.

Is living in the chapter house required?

No. Membership does not require you to live in the house. Many brothers live off-campus or in the dorms.

Can I rush if I'm not a freshman?

Absolutely. We accept rushees from all class years including transfer students.
