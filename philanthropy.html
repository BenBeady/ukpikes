Our Mission

Service Is a Core Value

The Omega Chapter of Pi Kappa Alpha believes in giving back — not just on campus, but in the greater Lexington community and beyond. Every semester, our brothers log hundreds of service hours and raise thousands of dollars for organizations that make a real difference.

Our philanthropy efforts are coordinated by our VP of Philanthropy and guided by Jeff Ashley of Pike National, who oversees our national fundraising partnerships.
